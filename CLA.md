## How to sign

When you open your first pull request, an automated check will ask you to sign.
Add a **single pull-request comment** with exactly the following text:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your signature (your GitHub username and the metadata of that comment) is then
recorded once on the `cla-signatures` branch and applies to all your future
Contributions to this Project. Maintainers and bots are exempt.
